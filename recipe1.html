Prague Cake

Introducing the Prague Cake, a sublime confection that encapsulates the very essence of indulgence in every delectable bite. Nestled within its modest dimensions lies a world of flavor, artistry, and tradition, skillfully blended to delight the senses.

At first glance, the Prague Cake reveals itself as a petite masterpiece, inviting you to explore its intricate layers. With a gentle touch, your fork sinks into the moist, tender sponge cake, as it unveils a symphony of tastes and textures. Rich, velvety chocolate ganache envelops each layer, harmonizing effortlessly with the light, airy crumb of the cake itself.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

50 min.

serving

8-10 servings

Instructions

step 1

Mix all ingredients for the dough and bake at 180 degrees for about 40 min.

step 2

Whisk the egg, add milk and flour. Heat in a saucepan over medium heat, stirring constantly until a thick mass forms and bubbles appear. Let it cool down.

step 3

Mix all ingredients for the cream #2.

step 4

Mix cream #1 with cream #2.

step 5

Cut the cake into 3-4 parts, coat each with cream. Spread cream on top of pie as well.

ingredients

For dough:

condensed milk - 1 can

eggs - 2

cocoa powder - 3 tbsp.

1 tsp soda with 1 tbsp. vinegar

For cream #1:

milk - 200 ml

egg - 1

flour - 2 tbsp.

For cream #2:

butter - 150 gr

sugar - 1 glass

cocoa powder - 3 tbsp.
