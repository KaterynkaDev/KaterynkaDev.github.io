Apricot Cake

You can add cinnamon, ground ginger, or nutmeg to this cake for a more aromatic flavor!

Preparation Time

15 min.

Cooking Time

60 min.

Servings

6

Recipe:

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.

Step 2

Mix the flour with the baking powder and salt.

Step 3

Beat the eggs with milk in a food processor at high speed.

Step 4

Add the softened heavy cream and continue mixing.

Step 5

While the food processor is running, add the flour with baking powder and 140g of sugar in portions.

Step 6

Add vanilla extract and mix the dough until it becomes uniform.

Step 7

Cut the apricots in half and remove the pits.

Step 8

Spread the dough in a large baking dish, level it, and arrange the apricots with the cut side up, gently pressing them into the dough.

Step 9

Place the dish in the preheated oven.

Step 10

After 25 minutes, open the oven, sprinkle the cake with the remaining sugar, and bake for another 10-15 minutes.

Step 11

Cool the cake in the dish, then remove it.

Ingredients

Apricots (filling) - 10 pcs

Sugar (filling) - 50 g

Wheat flour - 185 g

Heavy cream - 160 g

Sugar - 140 g

Eggs - 3 pcs

Baking powder - 1/2 tsp

Salt - a pinch

Vanilla - 1 tsp
