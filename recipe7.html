Chocolate Cookies

Simple, delicious, light, soft, and perfect for an evening tea! This treat will undoubtedly be loved by your family and make a great addition to the table for family or friendly gatherings.

Preparation Time

10 min.

Cooking Time

40 min.

Servings

6

Recipe:

Step 1

Combine dry ingredients: sift flour with cocoa powder, add baking powder, and salt.

Step 2

Beat eggs with sugar until a fluffy foam forms, about 5 minutes. Add room temperature butter. Add vanilla. Mix well.

Step 3

Gradually add dry ingredients. Cover the resulting dough with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour.

Step 4

Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius.

Step 5

Cover the baking sheet with parchment paper. Scoop the dough onto the sheet using a teaspoon.

Step 6

Bake for about 8 minutes. Let it cool slightly on the baking sheet for a few minutes as it will be too soft. Remove from the oven.

Ingredients

Wheat flour - 2 cups

Butter - 150 g

Sugar - 1 cup

Eggs - 2 pcs

Baking powder - 1 tsp

Cocoa powder - 1.2 cups

Vanilla - 1 tsp
