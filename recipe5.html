Fruit & Berry Pie

An incredibly airy, delicate pie with berries and fruits.

Preparation Time

10 min.

Cooking Time

50 min.

Servings

6-8

Sugar Cookie Recipe:

Step 1

Whisk the eggs with sugar until they turn white. Add oil and kefir, continue beating, add the baking powder and flour, beat until you get a uniform consistency. The dough should be elastic and resemble thick cream, flowing like a ribbon.

Step 2

Pour the dough into a mold - I use a silicone one, which I lightly grease with oil (although it's not necessary).

Step 3

On top of the dough, place a dense layer of fruits and berries - any that you have available. The key is to slice them thinly so they are light and don't hinder the dough from rising.

Step 4

Place it in the oven at 170-180 degrees Celsius for 1 to 1 hour and 10 minutes, depending on your oven. I have a convection oven with top and bottom heating elements. Check for readiness with a wooden stick.

Step 5

Add the dry ingredients to the previously mixed mixture with sugar and eggs. Mix until you form a dough.

Step 6

You can dust the finished pie with powdered sugar if desired.

Ingredients

Eggs - 3 pcs

Sugar - 200 g

Butter, room temperature (or baking margarine) - 70 g

Kefir (3.2% is preferred, and you can also try replacing kefir with sour milk, drinkable yogurt, or whey from cottage cheese) - 180-200 g

Wheat flour - 250-270 g

Baking powder - 2-2.5 tsp

Fruits and berries for the filling, and a small amount of sugar for dusting if desired.
