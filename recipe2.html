Omelet

Start your day with a burst of flavor and protein-packed goodness by indulging in our Cheesy Tomato Omelette. This simple yet satisfying breakfast classic combines the creamy richness of eggs and milk with the savory delight of melted cheese and the fresh, juicy goodness of ripe tomatoes. Whether you're looking for a quick weekday breakfast or a leisurely weekend brunch, this omelette is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you energized for the day ahead.

With just a few basic ingredients and easy-to-follow steps, you'll have a delightful omelette ready in no time. Plus, you can customize it with your favorite cheese and herbs to make it your own.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

2 servings

Instructions

step 1

Prepare the ingredients: Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add milk to the eggs and whisk them together until well combined. Dice the tomato. Shred the cheese. Season with a pinch of salt and a dash of pepper.

step 2

Heat the pan: Place a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium heat. Add the butter or cooking oil and let it melt and coat the bottom of the pan evenly.

step 3

Pour the Egg Mixture: Once the butter is hot and slightly bubbly (but not browned), pour the whisked egg and milk mixture into the pan.

step 4

Once the butter is hot and slightly bubbly (but not browned), pour the whisked egg and milk mixture into the pan.

step 5

As the eggs begin to set around the edges, use a spatula to gently lift them and tilt the pan to let the uncooked eggs flow to the edges.

step 6

Sprinkle the shredded cheese evenly over one half of the omelette. Add the diced tomatoes on top of the cheese.

step 7

Let the omelette cook for another minute or so until the cheese inside melts and the eggs are fully set. You can cover the pan with a lid to help the cheese melt faster if desired.

step 8

Carefully slide the cooked omelette onto a plate. You can use the spatula to assist with this.

step 9

Garnish and Serve: Garnish your cheese and tomato omelette with fresh herbs like parsley or chives if you like. Season with a bit more salt and pepper to taste, if needed.

step 10

Enjoy: Serve your delicious omelette hot and enjoy a delightful combination of cheesy, creamy eggs, and the freshness of diced tomatoes.

Ingredients

eggs - 2

milk - 2 tbsp.

shredded cheese (such as cheddar, mozzarella, or your choice) - 1/4 cup

tomato - 1, diced

Salt and pepper to taste

1 tablespoon butter or cooking oil

Fresh herbs (optional, for garnish)
