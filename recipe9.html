Hamburgers

If you love fast food but don't want to spend too much, why not learn to make delicious burgers at home? First, it's much more economical; second, they taste just as good; and third, with your homemade burger, you can put whatever you want according to your culinary preferences.

Preparation Time

15 min.

Cooking Time

60 min.

Servings

6

Recipe:

Step 1

Cut the beef and beef fat into small pieces and pass them through a meat grinder.

Step 2

Add salt and ground black pepper to the minced meat. Mix everything. Form 10 flat burgers with the minced meat and refrigerate them for 30 minutes.

Step 3

Cut the hamburger buns in half and fry them in butter until a light crust forms.

Step 4

In a small bowl, mix mayonnaise and mustard.

Step 5

After 30 minutes, take the flat burgers out of the refrigerator and fry them in a small amount of oil in a hot pan. Fry the burgers for 3-4 minutes on one side. It's preferable to press the burgers with a spatula or your hand while frying.

Step 6

Flip the burgers and place a slice of processed cheese on the cooked side. Fry the burgers for another 4 minutes to make the bottom crispy.

Step 7

Now, let's start assembling our burgers. Generously spread the bottom half of the buns with mayonnaise and mustard sauce.

Step 8

On the sauce, place thin slices of sweet red onion.

Step 9

On top of the onions, place thin slices of pickles.

Step 10

Now, place a cheeseburger on top.

Step 11

On the cheeseburger, place a fresh tomato slice.

Step 12

Place lettuce leaves on top of the tomato.

Step 13

Spread ketchup on the top half of the buns and cover them with the burgers.

Step 14

Serve the burgers with cold drinks. Enjoy!

Ingredients

Hamburger buns - 10 units

Beef fat - 150 g

Iceberg lettuce - half a head

Pickles - 10 units

Beef - 1 kg

Tomato - 2 units

Red onion - 2 units

Processed cheese slices - 20 units

Mayonnaise - 2 tablespoons

Salt - 1 teaspoon

Butter - 100 g

Mustard (prepared) - 1 tablespoon

Ketchup - 2 tablespoons

Ground black pepper - a pinch

Vegetable oil - 100 ml
