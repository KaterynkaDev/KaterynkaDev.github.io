Chocolate-banana cake

Chocolate banana cake is a real pleasure in every bite. Its rich taste and texture combine the best features of chocolate and sweet banana. A tender biscuit made from juicy bananas makes the cake soft and moist.

The chocolate ganache enveloping each layer gives it a rich and aromatic taste, as well as tenderness and richness of taste. This cake is a real treat for fans of chocolate and bananas, which have come together to create an unforgettable sweet symphony of taste.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

80 min.

serving

8 servings

Instructions

step 1

Beat eggs with sugar, salt, and vanilla for 5 minutes.

step 2

Dissolve coffee in warm milk.

step 3

Slightly warm the butter and add it to milk, baking soda to the egg mixture, and mix all well.

step 4

Mix flour with cocoa, sift into the batter, and stir.

step 5

Pour into a greased 20 cm (8-inch) baking pan.

step 6

Bake at 170°C (340°F) for 30-50 minutes (mine took 40 minutes; check your oven, and don't open it for the first 20 minutes; test with a dry toothpick). Let it cool.

step 7

For the Cream: Mix sour cream with sugar.

step 7

For the Glaze: Mix sugar, cocoa powder and sour cream and simmer over low heat until smooth (not for long).

step 8

Cut the cooled sponge cake into 2-3 cake layers

step 9

Grease the bottom and middle cake with cream, layer with bananas, stack on top of each other. Grease the top crust, but do not add bananas.

ingredients

eggs - 3 pcs

sugar - 1 cup

milk - 100 ml

butter - 90 gr

vanillin - a pinch

salt - a pinch

instant coffee - 1 tbsp.

cocoa powder - 1.5-2 tbsp.

soda with vinegar - 1.5-2 tbsp.

sour cream - 500-600 gr.

sugar - 3 tbsp.

cocoa powder - 1 tbsp.

bananas - 2-4 pcs.
