Sugar Cookies

Sugar cookies are the simplest and most delicious way to sweeten your day. With this recipe, you will get tender, sweet cookies that melt in your mouth and leave a pleasant taste in your memory.

These cookies impress with their tenderness and crispiness at the same time. With a vanilla aroma and a refined sugar flavor, they are perfect for accompanying a cup of tea or coffee. While making these cookies, you can enjoy the scent of fresh baking in your kitchen and share this delicate dessert with your loved ones.

Preparation Time

10 min.

Cooking Time

20 min.

Servings

4

Sugar Cookie Recipe:

Step 1

Store all ingredients and the utensils you will use at room temperature.

Step 2

In a large bowl, mix 1 cup of sugar and 1 cup of butter until you get a smooth cream.

Step 3

Add 3 eggs and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract. Beat until the ingredients are combined.

Step 4

In another bowl, mix 2 teaspoons of baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda, and 2 1/2 cups of flour.

Step 5

Add the dry ingredients to the mixture of sugar and eggs. Mix until the dough forms.

Step 6

Cover the dough with plastic wrap and leave it in the refrigerator for an hour.

Step 7

Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) before baking the dough for about 10 minutes until it becomes golden.

Step 8

Remove the dough from the refrigerator and shape small balls. Place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving gaps between them.

Step 9

Bake the sugar cookies in a preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until they have a golden crust.

Step 10

Remove the sugar cookies from the oven and let them cool on a rack before serving.

Ingredients

Sugar - 1 cup

Butter - 1 cup

Eggs - 3 pcs

Vanilla Extract - 1 tsp

Baking Powder - 2 tsp

Baking Soda - 1/2 tsp

Flour - 2 1/2 cups
