Blueberry Pie

Simple, delicious, light, soft, and perfect for an evening tea! This treat will undoubtedly be loved by your family and make a great addition to the table for family or friendly gatherings.

Preparation Time

15 min.

Cooking Time

70 min.

Servings

4-6

Recipe:

Step 1

First, prepare the necessary ingredients as per the list. Use high-quality flour. Use large eggs. If using small eggs, use 3. Butter should be at room temperature, so take it out of the fridge in advance.

Step 2

In a bowl, combine room-temperature softened butter with sugar. Beat everything with a mixer until fluffy for 2-3 minutes. Optionally, you can add a pinch of vanilla for flavor.

Step 3

Gradually add eggs, beating the mixture each time. The result should be a fluffy and uniform mass.

Step 4

Sift the flour with baking powder and salt, then add it to the dough. Use a mixer on low speed. Check the consistency of the dough. It should be smooth, soft, but thick.

Step 5

Now, prepare the necessary ingredients for the filling. You can use fresh or frozen blueberries. You don't need to thaw frozen berries in advance. You can also replace blueberries with any other berries or a mix of your choice.

Step 6

Sort and rinse the blueberries and pat them dry with paper towels. Add sugar to the blueberries and mix.

Step 7

Grease a baking dish (Ø 20 cm) with butter and line it with parchment paper. If you're using a silicone mold, you don't need to grease it. Spread the dough evenly in the baking dish.

Step 8

Spoon the blueberries onto the dough. You can place them in heaps like I did, or evenly distribute them over the entire dough.

Step 9

Bake the pie in a preheated oven at 180 °C for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick comes out dry. Remove the finished pie from the oven and leave it in the mold for 5 minutes.

Step 10

Then transfer the pie to a plate, optionally dust it with powdered sugar, and serve. Enjoy your meal!

Ingredients

Wheat flour - 160 g

Butter - 120 g

Sugar - 100 g

Eggs - 3 pcs

Baking powder - 1 tsp

Salt - 1 g

Blueberries - 100 g

Sugar - 2 tbsp
